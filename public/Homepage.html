Welcome to the Diabetes TrackrS.

The Diabetes TrackrS is an app designed to help those with either Type 1 or Type 2 diabetes
keep track of various health statistics as well as the medications they are taking.
With TrackrS you can log and keep track of the following data:
- Blood glucose and blood pressure.
- Carbohydrate intake. Includes a handy search engine that helps find nutritional information on common foods.
- Any prescribed medications. Includes dosage and number of times a day they are taken.
